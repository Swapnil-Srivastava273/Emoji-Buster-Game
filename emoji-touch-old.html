Instructions

It all started on WhatsApp. An emoji (to be refered as 'this guy' in the rest of the conversation) started eating other emojis, roaming aimlessly and making trouble for users.

You need to stop this guy ->

After every certain interval of time, an emoji item will appear on your screen. This guy will run towards it, aiming to consume it. You can prevent this by clicking him before he beomes star-eyed (). If you are able to prevent him from getting the item, you get it.

Each item is there for some specific task. The food items will add to your score if collected and do nothing if missed. The following table lists the food items with their score:

Item Score
+500
+300
+250
+400

A money bag() gives you +1000 points if collected and -1000 points if missed. If your score falls below -1000, it will be reset to zero and you will lose a life.

A heart() denotes a life. On collecting, it gives you an additional life. If you miss it, you lose a life. The game is over when you lose all your lives.

A crystal ball() denotes 'Foresight'. Collecting it gives you 'Foresight Time'. It is the time duration for which you will be able to look at this guy's scary plans as he moves. Missing a crystal ball reduces your 'Foresight Time'.

Don't let this guy create problems. Good luck.
